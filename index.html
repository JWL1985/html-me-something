Pros & Cons of Being a Real Estate Agent

Real Estate is a great investment, and something we ALL need. whether you rent or own, we all need a roof over our head. Just because industry is huge doesn't mean its easy for an agent to make a living. In this article I'll go over some pros & cons about being a Real Estate Agent in 2019.

PROS

  • Potential to Make A LOT of $$$
  • Flexible Schedule
  • You Are Your Own Boss
  • You Get to See Some Really Cool Houses

CONS

  • No Guaranteed Pay Day
  • Market Volatility
  • VERY Competitive
  • Prospecting for New Clients Sucks!
