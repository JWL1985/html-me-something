Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have to take classes to get your real estate license in Missouri?

YES! First-time licensee candidates are required to take and pass two pre-licensing courses.

  • 48-Hour Missouri Salesperson Pre-Examination Course (Must be completed before the licensing exam)
  • 24-Hour Missouri Real Estate Practice Course (Must be taken after the first course but can also be taken after the exam)
  • Must pass final classroom exam with score of 75% or higher.

Is there any other tests besides the classroom exam?

YES! There is a state exam, which you can't take until after you have a valid certification showing you have completed the required 72 hours of classroom work.

  • Cost of Test: $68
  • Testing Locations:
    • Location 1: St. Louis 11100 Mueller Road Suite 7 & 8 St Louis, MO 63123
    • Location 2: Kansas City PSI Services (PSI) 18000 W 105th Street Olathe, KS 66061
    • Location 3: Springfield PSI Services (PSI) 3003 East Chestnut Expressway RDI Building Suite 400 Springfield, Missouri
    • Location 4: Jefferson City Suite C 2410 Hyde Park Road Jefferson City, Missouri
    • Location 5: Kansas City - Overland Park, KS 8001 College Blvd, Suite 285 Overland Park, KS 66210

How Long Does It Take To Get Your License?

The reqired classroom work takes 2 weeks, plus another few days to complete the 24hr online course. After that, you can study and take the state exam as soon as you are ready. If you choose to complete the required 72 hrs of classroom work online, rather than the in-class setting, you can go at your own pace but for most people it usually takes a little longer to complete online classes.

What if I fail the State exam?

You can retake the test as many times as you want but it costs $68 each time.

What is the total cost of everything needed to get real estate license?

  • 72 hr classroom work: Approx $500 depending on where you take classes.
  • License Application & Issuance: $90 total ($50 application fee & $40 issuance fee.)
  • Fingerprint/Background Check: $43.05
  • Joining Your Local Board of REALTORS: This cost will vary depending on which board you join but the cost can range anywhere from $500 to $1500 initiation fee. Most boards also have monthly dues of $20-$50/month and some even have an annual fee up to $500/year in addition to the monthly fees.
